🏃
Trish: morning run — Victoria Embankment, flat Thames path, stunning views
⚡
Tour for Muggles — starts at Leadenhall Market. Rated 4.9 ⭐. Guide "Harry Trotter" is legendary. 2.5 hours. Book ahead.

Leadenhall Market — Diagon Alley / Leaky Cauldron exterior. Stunning Victorian painted ironwork.
🌉
Millennium Bridge — destroyed by Death Eaters in Half-Blood Prince. Walk across toward the South Bank.
🎨
Tate Modern — right at the foot of the bridge. Free entry to the permanent collection. The Turbine Hall alone is worth the stop. 1–2 hours.
🍮
The Anchor Bankside — 2-min walk from Tate Modern. Historic Thames pub, sticky toffee pudding confirmed on the menu.
